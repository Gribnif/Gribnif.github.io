Classic Atari ST/TT/Falcon Software

Started in 1988, Gribnif Software produced various titles for the Atari ST line of computers.

This site is dedicated to preserving two of those titles: NeoDesk 4, the desktop replacement, and Geneva, the multitasking environment. The site is maintained by Dan Wilga, the original author of the programs.

News

2/11/2018: NeoDesk 4 and Geneva are now available as freeware in the Downloads section. We hope to make the source code available in the coming months.
